The **Intel** tab is Conway's library of **entities** — every real-world thing Conway has identified across runs, with a dossier you can read. People, accounts, organizations, locations, and typologies (recurring behavior patterns like *ATO via SIM swap*) all live here.

## The entity list

Search the list by name, ID, or attribute at the top of the tab. Narrow by type (Person, Org., Account, Location, Typology) using the type dropdown if the name alone is too broad. Counters at the top of the page tell you how many entities are tracked overall and how many are new this week.

## Dossiers

Click any entity to open its dossier. You'll see a short description Conway has built up about the entity, the investigations it has appeared in (each with its verdict), any intelligence the agent has saved about it, and its connections to other entities — the account that belongs to a person, the person tied to a location, and so on.

## Walkthrough: researching an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Intel">
    From Detection, click the **Intel** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter to accounts">
    Pick **Account** from the type dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search">
    Type the account ID or name in the search field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the dossier">
    Click the row. You'll see every investigation this account has appeared in and every piece of intelligence Conway has saved about it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask follow-up questions">
    Drag the entity card onto the chat pane.
  </Step>
</Steps>
