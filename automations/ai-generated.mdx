Instead of building a playbook from scratch, describe what you want in plain English and let Conway draft it. Click **GENERATE** on the [Automations](/automations/overview) page (or inside the [Automations tab](/response/automations-tab) in Response) to open the generation prompt.

## GENERATE vs. CREATE

Use **GENERATE** when you have a policy or intent in words, not yet a worked sequence — or when you're not sure which phase types exist and want to see one of them in context. Use **CREATE** when you already know exactly which phases to include and how they should route.

## The prompt

Specific prompts produce specific playbooks; vague prompts produce vague ones. In the prompt, tell Conway:

- **What triggers the playbook** — *"when a case reaches Escalated"*, *"every morning at 06:00"*, *"on demand from chat"*.
- **What it should do** — the sequence of checks and actions, in order.
- **What outcomes should end it** — under what condition it should escalate, resolve, or hand off.

Write it the way you'd explain the workflow to a new teammate.

## Reviewing the draft

Conway drops you into the builder with the generated playbook marked **Draft** and **Unsaved**. Don't rush to publish. Click through every phase to inspect its configuration, adjust gates that don't match your intent, add missing phases, remove extras, and save as you go. Only click **PUBLISH** once you've read every phase and are comfortable with what will actually run.

## Walkthrough: generate and publish

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the generate prompt">
    Click **GENERATE** on the Automations page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe what you want">
    Write the trigger, the steps, and the end conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the draft">
    Open every phase; confirm each configuration matches your intent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and save">
    Adjust anything that's off. Click **SAVE** to persist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **PUBLISH** to take the playbook Live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
In **Pilot deployments**, run generated playbooks past your Conway contact before publishing. See [Pilot limitations](/pilot/limitations).
</Note>
