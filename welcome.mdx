Conway helps you detect anomalies in your data and investigate them. This guide walks you through every screen in the Conway application so you always have a reference for what each button, tab, and field does.

## Who this guide is for

Fraud analysts and other everyday users of Conway. No engineering background required.

## How this guide is organized

- **Getting started** — the lay of the land: navigation, core concepts.
- **Detection** — the main hub where runs are created and investigations are reviewed.
- **Run detail** — what to look at when you open a single run.
- **Response** — working cases end-to-end.
- **Case detail** — the single-case workspace.
- **Automations** — building and editing playbooks.
- **Sources** — connecting to your data warehouse.
- **Chat assistant** — using Conway's built-in assistant.

The **Pilot** tab (top of the page) contains information specific to your deployment — who to contact, which sources are already set up, and any preset playbooks.
